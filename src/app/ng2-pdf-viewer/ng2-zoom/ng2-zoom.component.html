ng2-pdf-viewer offers several zoom modes:

  • [zoom] is a numerical value setting the scaling factor.
  • [zoom-scale] may have the values 'page-width', 'page-height' or 'page-fit'. The size of the container determines the scaling factor.
  • [original-size]="true" is the same as [zoom]="1"
  • [fit-to-page] works in combination with [original-size]="true". You can show your document in original size, and make sure that it's not bigger then container block.
  • [autoresize]="true" resizes the zoom when the size of the container changes.
