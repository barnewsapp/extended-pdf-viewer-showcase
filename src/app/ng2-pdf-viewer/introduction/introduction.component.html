What is ng2-pdf-viewer?

ng2-pdf-viewer is a no-nonsense PDF viewer for Angular. It displays your PDF file, but nothing else. If you need a UI, you'll need to write it yourself. In other words: it's a very flexible PDF viewer with a low footprint.

Licence and market share

ng2-pdf-viewer has been published under a MIT license. It's got the biggest market share among the open source PDF viewers for Angular.
