The flag [enableRelativeCoords]="true" improves the pinch gesture on mobile devices. By default, pinching simple increases or decreases the zoom factor without considering the position of your fingers. As a result, the text you want to enlarge moves away from your focus. Activating [enableRelativeCoords] fixes this. It aims at not moving the word you're interested in. In other words, it aims at zooming just the way you expect it.

Caveat: This is an experimental feature. As simple as the requirement is, it turns out to be surprisingly difficult once you're trying to implement it. I don't believe we've already considered every corner case. If you observe a bug, please report it at https://github.com/stephanrauh/ngx-extended-pdf-viewer/issues.
