You can draw the PDF file on a background of your choice.

Handle with care! PDF files are - by design - files you can trust. The reader expects them not to be manipulated. Modifiying the background of the PDF file is just that. You're displaying the file in a way the author didn't want you to do. That's the reason why ngx-extended-pdf-viewer always shows a fat red warning telling the reader what's going on. I ask you not to remove this message. If you do, you might undermine trust in the PDF file format. Please don't do that!

[pdfBackgroundColor]="undefined" (or just omit it)
pdfBackgroundColor="'#e8e1c4'"
pdfBackgroundColor ="colorByPage()" (sourcecode on the right hand side)
pdfBackgroundColor="oldPaper()" (sourcecode on the right hand side)
