By default, the pinch gesture simple zooms the entire page on mobile devices. If you want to zoom the PDF file without zooming the rest of the page, you can activate the enablePinchOnMobile option.

enablePinchOnMobile doesn't modify the behavior of laptops. Their touchpads translate the finger gestures to mouse wheel events. This approach works well enough, so the PDF viewer doesn't add additional support for touch gestures on laptop touchpads. In other words, enablePinchOnMobile is ignored on laptops.

However, this showcase allows you to emululate touch gestures using your mouse or trackpad. Hit the ALT (or OPTION on new Macs) key twice within a second to toggle between the mouse mode and the tough gesture mode. Hold the SHIFT key, click, and move the move to simulate a pinch gesture.

The double-tap (or double-click) gesture enlarges the PDF file. By default, the target zoom is "page-width". You can define a different target zoom by setting pdfDefaultOptions.doubleTapZoomFactor. In this demo, that's 125%.
